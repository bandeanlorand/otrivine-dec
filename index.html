How it works

Otrivine Adult Metered Dose 0.1% Nasal Spray delivers an exact dose of medicated spray inside the nose. It provides up to 10 hours of relief in as little as 2 minutes. It contains the active ingredient xylometazoline hydrochloride that helps to open up and clear nasal passages by reducing nasal mucus and returning swollen blood vessels to their normal size

How to use

For adults and children over 12 years of age, spray once in each nostril 1 or 3 times daily.

Detailed instructions:

  1. Clear your nose.
  2. Lean forward slightly and insert the nozzle into a nostril.
  3. Depress the pump once and sniff at the same time.
  4. Repeat for the other nostril.

Do not use continuously for more than 7 days. If symptoms persist consult your doctor or pharmacist. To avoid possible spread of infection, each pack should be used by only one person.

Ingredients

The active ingredient is xylometazoline hydrochloride (0.1% w/v). The other ingredients are benzalkonium chloride, sodium phosphate, disodium edetate, sodium acid phosphate, sodium chloride, sorbitol, hypromellose and purified water.

Further information

Otrivine Adult Metered Dose 0.1% Nasal Spray is available from your pharmacist in 10 ml packs. Keep out of reach of children. Do not use after the expiry date stated on the bottle. Do not store above 25ºC. We do not recommend using each bottle past 28 days of opening.
