What is it?

Otrivin Natural Plus with Eucalyptus is a unique natural formulation which combines the natural decongestant properties of sea water with eucalyptus and wild mint. It quickly provides natural relief from nasal congestion due to colds and rhinosinusitis to help you breathe freely again. It also has a purifying action to clear nasal passages and help to remove bacteria and viruses.

How does it work?

During a cold the inflammatory process results in the swelling of the nasal mucus, blocked nose and increased mucus. The hypertonic* sea water in Otrivin Natural Plus with Eucalyptus creates a pressure difference between the solution and the mucous membrane (osmotic effect), helping to reduce the swelling of the nasal passages. The product also has a purifying action which clears the nasal passages helping to remove mucus trapped bacteria, viruses and impurities from the nose.

Furthermore, the essential oil of eucalyptus and extract of wild mint provide a fresh sensation in the nasal passages, giving immediate sensation of increased aiflow and relief.

Otrivin Natural Plus with Eucalyptus:
  • Relieves nasal congestion in less than 3 minutes
  • Has a purifying action to clear the nasal passages
  • Helps to remove bacteria and viruses

* Hypertonic sea water solutions differ from isotonic sea water solutions as the sea salt concentration is optimized at 2.2% to effectively relieve nasal congestion. This concentration also ensures that the solution is gentle enough to be compatible with the nasal mucosa.

What are seawater's natural benefits?

Most saline solutions are created by adding purified water to dilute the sea water or by simply adding sodium chloride (salt) to purified water. The natural sea water in Otrivin Natural Plus with Eucalyptus undergoes a process which simply lowers the amount of salt but still retains the richness of all the important minerals and microelements contained in sea water, such as calcium, magnesium, iron and zinc rather than diluting these important oligoelements. The sea water is also filtered, sterilized and ozonated to ensure compatibility with the nasal mucosa.

How to use?

For adults and children over 6 years of age, spray once in each nostril up to 6 times a day. Before use please read carefully the full instructions for use enclosed in the package of Otrivin Natural Plus with Eucalyptus.

Directions for use

  1. Blow the nose.
  2. Shake the bottle before use.
  3. Remove the protective cap of the spray, see Figure 1.
  4. Before using for the first time, prime the pump by performing a few pumping motions until an even spray is released into the air. Be careful not to spray in the eyes or mouth.
  5. Hold the bottle upright with thumb under base and nozzle between two fingers.
  6. Lean the head forward slightly and insert the nozzle into the nostril, see Figure 2.
  7. Spray while breathing and breathe in gently through the nose.
  8. Repeat in the other nostril.
  9. Allow the product to act for a few minutes. Do not blow the nose during this time, but wipe it if necessary.
  10. Then, blow the nose if needed.
For hygienic reasons, in order to avoid contamination:
  • After each use, wash the nozzle with hot water, dry it and put the protective cap back on.
  • The spray should only be used by one person.

Pack size

Ingredients

Sea water (hypertonic at 2.2% sea salt concentration), essential oil of Eucalyptus globulus extract of wild mint.
